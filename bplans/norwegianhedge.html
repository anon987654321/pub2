Norwegian Hedge Fund Business Plan

A specialized hedge fund focusing on Nordic markets, renewable energy, and sustainable investments with AI-powered trading strategies.

  • Nordic equity markets focus
  • Renewable energy and green technology
  • Oil and gas sector expertise
  • Quantitative trading algorithms
  • ESG-compliant investment criteria

Market Focus
